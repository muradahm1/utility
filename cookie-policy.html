Last Updated: July 22, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how GetCalcu uses them, and how you can control them. For broader privacy information, see our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners about how their site is being used. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

How We Use Cookies

We use three categories of cookies:

Category Purpose Can be disabled?
Essential Required for the site to function. Stores your theme preference (dark/light mode) and your cookie consent choice. No personal data is collected. No — these are necessary for basic functionality.
Analytics Helps us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., which tools are popular, what devices are used). Data is anonymized and aggregated via Google Analytics 4. No PII is collected. Yes — choose "Essential Only" in the consent banner.
Advertising Used by third-party ad networks (e.g., Google AdSense) to serve relevant ads. These partners may use cookies to track visits across websites. Yes — choose "Essential Only" in the consent banner.

Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit GetCalcu, a consent banner appears at the bottom of the screen. You can choose:

  • Accept All — enables all cookie categories including analytics and advertising.
  • Essential Only — disables analytics and advertising cookies. Only strictly necessary cookies are set.
